"Elvi Stone" is a company, which is specialized on manufacturing and installation of marble and granite products. found in 2002, company has already gained a reputation of one of the leader fabricators in metamorphic rock industry. As for today, our production has broaden up to making of marble, granite, onyx, travertine and quartzite products.

 

A wide variety of possible unique patterns enables to create exclusive designer interior pieces, which don't need any specific maintenance. Considering this you won't have to worry about the appearance of our products even in 1 or 2 years time as they will look as spectacular as on the day of setting. In our manufacture we use only highest quality natural stones and rocks represent luxury brand materials. One of the most beneficial characteristics of our company is highly professional consultants, architects, designers and other employees, whose main purpose is to make your house even better and more fashionable.

 

"Elvi Stone's" employees always treat every client in an individual and unique way to make your dreams and wishes come true. In the end you are not only getting a stylish piece of interior, but an exclusive design, which emphasizes your individuality. From the order to the final product, our consultant will take care of the quality of work and will always be there to answer all of your questions. Our company takes full responsibility of adhering to all the established time limits of manufacturing process and the outcome of the products.
